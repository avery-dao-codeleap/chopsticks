# Chopsticks Marketing Plan

*Last Updated: February 3, 2026*

---

## Marketing Overview

### Goals by Phase

| Phase | Marketing Goal | Key Metric |
|-------|----------------|------------|
| Pre-Launch | Build waitlist, establish brand | 500+ waitlist signups |
| MVP Launch | Acquire first 100 users, seed activity | 100 MAU, 50 requests/day |
| Post-Validation | Grow to 1,000 users, build community | 1,000 MAU, strong retention |
| Phase 1+ | Scale acquisition, expand cities | 10,000+ users |

### Target Audience Segments

| Segment | Description | Channels | Messaging |
|---------|-------------|----------|-----------|
| **Expats** | Foreigners living in HCMC | Facebook groups, coworking spaces | "Meet locals, discover real food" |
| **Travelers** | Visitors to Vietnam | Hostels, travel blogs, TikTok | "Eat with locals, not tourists" |
| **Locals (English-speaking)** | Young Vietnamese professionals | Instagram, TikTok, universities | "Meet interesting people over food" |
| **Food enthusiasts** | Anyone who loves food | Food influencers, foodie groups | "Discover the best hidden gems" |

1. Follow and engage with their content genuinely
2. DM with personal note + beta invite
3. Offer: Early access + feature their favorite spots in curated lists
4. Ask: Post about the app when it launches

## City Expansion Marketing

*Template for launching new cities*

### Pre-Launch (New City)

1. **Research phase:**
   - Identify local food influencers
   - Map restaurant scene
   - Find community groups

2. **Content phase:**
   - Create city-specific curated lists
   - Build local Instagram content backlog
   - Recruit local food experts as advisors

3. **Seeding phase:**
   - Waitlist for new city
   - Influencer outreach
   - Community engagement

### Launch (New City)

- Localized launch announcement
- City-specific influencer campaign
- Cross-promotion from existing user base
- "Travel planning" angle for existing users

## Paid Acquisition (Future)

*Only after organic growth validated*

### Facebook/Instagram Ads

**Audiences:**
- Lookalike of existing users
- Interest: Food, dining, travel, expat life
- Location: HCMC (expand to new cities)
- Age: 22-40

**Creative testing:**
- Food-first (delicious dish imagery)
- Social-first (people eating together)
- Discovery-first (hidden gem reveal)
- Founder story

**Budget:** Start with $500/month, scale based on CAC

### Influencer Paid Partnerships

**When:** After proving organic influencer ROI

**Structure:**
- Paid posts with affiliate tracking
- Revenue share on signups
- Exclusive list collaborations
